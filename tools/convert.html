Frequently asked questions

Can I preserve transparency?

PNG and WebP can preserve transparency. JPG does not support transparency and may add a solid background.

Can this convert to SVG?

The SVG option wraps the raster image for compatibility. It does not automatically vectorize a bitmap into editable paths.

Which format should I use for websites?

WebP is often a strong choice for modern websites. Use PNG for transparent graphics and JPG for broad photo compatibility.
