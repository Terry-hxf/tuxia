Tool Guide

QR Code Generator Guide and FAQ

Create custom QR codes for links, text, contact snippets, Wi-Fi notes, labels, packaging drafts, and quick sharing. The generator includes live preview, size controls, error correction, colors, dot style, corner style, optional logo embedding, and PNG or SVG export.

Best for

  • Generate QR codes from URLs, plain text, or short messages.
  • Customize foreground color, background color, pattern style, and size.
  • Export QR codes as PNG or SVG for digital and print workflows.

How to use it

  1. Enter the text or link you want to encode.
  2. Adjust size, error correction, colors, style, and optional logo.
  3. Preview the QR code and download it in your preferred format.
Privacy note: QR code generation runs locally in the browser. The text you enter is not sent to a TUXIA server.

Frequently asked questions

Which QR code format should I download?

Use PNG for quick sharing and raster graphics. Use SVG when you need a scalable vector file for design or print.

Can I add a logo to the QR code?

Yes. You can upload a small logo and tune error correction to keep the QR code readable.

Why should I test a QR code after export?

Color contrast, logo size, print quality, and error correction can affect scanning. Always test the final file with a phone camera.
