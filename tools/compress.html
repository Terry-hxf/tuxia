Compress images online with a before-and-after preview. TUXIA lets you adjust quality, compare the original and compressed file, and export common image formats for websites, email, documents, social posts, and lightweight publishing.

Privacy note: Compression runs in your browser with Canvas APIs. The selected image is not uploaded to a TUXIA server.

Frequently asked questions

Why can a compressed image become larger?

Some images are already optimized, and converting between formats can add overhead. Try WebP or reduce quality further when file size matters.

Which format is best for smaller files?

WebP often produces small files for photos and mixed graphics. JPG is widely compatible for photos, while PNG is better for transparency and sharp graphics.

Will compression reduce quality?

Lossy formats can reduce quality. Use the preview to balance file size and visual clarity.
