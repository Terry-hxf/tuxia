Tool Guide

Image Watermark Tool Guide and FAQ

Add text or image watermarks to pictures in your browser. Choose single placement, one-row, two-row, three-row, or full-screen watermark layouts, adjust size, opacity, rotation, color, and export the final protected image.

Best for

  • Add brand text, author names, draft labels, or copyright notes.
  • Apply a single watermark or repeated full-screen watermark pattern.
  • Use a text watermark or upload a small image watermark.

How to use it

  1. Upload the image you want to protect.
  2. Enter watermark text or choose a watermark image.
  3. Tune layout, size, opacity, rotation, color, and download.
Privacy note: Watermarking runs locally in the browser. Your selected images are not uploaded to a TUXIA server.

Frequently asked questions

Can I create full-screen repeated watermarks?

Yes. Choose the full-screen layout to repeat the watermark across the image.

Can I use a logo as a watermark?

Yes. Upload an image watermark and adjust its placement and opacity.

Can watermarks be removed?

Any visible watermark can sometimes be edited out. Use placement, opacity, and repetition based on the protection level you need.
