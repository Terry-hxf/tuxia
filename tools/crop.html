Tool Guide

Image Cropper Guide and FAQ

Crop images with a visual canvas, freeform selection, preset aspect ratios, and rotation controls. The cropper is useful for product photos, social media images, avatars, banners, screenshots, and focused composition adjustments.

Best for

  • Crop screenshots, photos, and product images.
  • Use common ratios such as 1:1, 4:3, 16:9, 3:4, or 9:16.
  • Rotate and preview the crop before exporting.

How to use it

  1. Upload an image and choose a crop ratio.
  2. Drag or adjust the crop area on the canvas.
  3. Preview and download the cropped image.
Privacy note: Cropping runs locally in your browser. TUXIA does not upload your selected image.

Frequently asked questions

Can I crop freely without a fixed ratio?

Yes. Choose the freeform option to draw any crop area.

Can I rotate before cropping?

Yes. Use the rotation controls before generating the final crop.

Will cropping change image quality?

Cropping removes pixels outside the selected area. Export format and quality settings can also affect the final file.
