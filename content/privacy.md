## How We Handle Your Images

Most TUXIA tools run directly in your browser, including Base64 conversion, QR code generation and decoding, image compression, format conversion, resizing, cropping, watermarking, local retouching, mosaic masking, color extraction, and EXIF viewing. Except for the Image URL Generator, the images you select are not uploaded to a TUXIA server.

The Image URL Generator uses the ImgBB API Key you provide and sends the selected image directly from your browser to ImgBB. Please make sure you have the right to upload and share the image, and keep your own API Key secure.

## Local Storage

TUXIA may use browser local storage to save a small amount of tool settings, such as dark mode preference, ImgBB API Key, and export format preferences. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

## Accounts

The GitHub static version does not include an account system. Tools are available directly without sign-in.

## Third-Party Services

If you enter an ImgBB API Key or other third-party configuration, related requests are made directly by your browser. Please review the privacy policy of the relevant third-party service and protect your credentials.

## Contact and Updates

We may update this policy as features change. The updated date at the top of this page will be adjusted when that happens.
